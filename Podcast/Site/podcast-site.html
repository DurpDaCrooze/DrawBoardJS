Saber wars

by Calvin Bussutill

Sabre Duels is a self-explanatory concept. Two people duel each other out in in a specific radius area. The point is to either knock off your opponent outside the ring or defeat them by having your Lightsabre making direct contact with their neck. All other Parts of the body do not register as a kill. They can still be hit to try and stun your opponent. But you will only win if you hit the neck or knock the opponent out of the area.
